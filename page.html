Gladiolus Flowers


RTS Flowers Had started with Gladiolus Flower in 2008. Now, we grow gladiolus in 20 acres. It is used for decoration in India. Netherlands is the largest exporter of gladiolus.


More

Gladiolus Bulbs


When the Gladiolus Sticks are cut, The Bulb is kept Underground for 4 weeks. After 4 weeks, the bulbs are uprooted and prosseced. The corms are separeted from them, the roots are peeled, they are counted and they are stored in cold Storage.
