How to grow Persian Buttercups: Sow Ranunculus Seeds in Winter In India. Ranunculus grows best in sandy soil. Ranunculus germinates after 30 days when sown from the seeds. The flowering starts after 4 months. The key to growing Ranunculus from flower seed is growing them in cool tempratures. Ranunculus needs to germinate in less then 20Â°C. Sprinkle ranunculus seeds generously 0.5 to 1 cm deep in the soil.

Ranunculus needs to be irrigated 3-4 times a day with sprinklers. It also depends on the weather. The weeds need to be controlled manually or by herbisides in order to make the best flowers and bulbs.
